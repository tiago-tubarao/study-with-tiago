Creator Inbox

A simple operating console for checking what students asked for, what needs fixing, and what to publish next.

This is a creator workflow page, not a password-protected dashboard. It is marked noindex and blocked in robots.txt, but anything on GitHub Pages can be opened by someone with the URL. Do not put private data, passwords, student identifiers, or paid analytics here.
