Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

What This Site Collects

Study with Tiago does not directly collect, store, or process any personal information.

This site has:

  • No user accounts or login
  • No cookies set by this site
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No contact forms
  • No email collection

Your quiz scores, confidence ratings, and feedback responses are stored only in your browser's local storage on your device. They are never sent to any server. You can clear them at any time by clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

This site links to or embeds content from the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data:

YouTube (Google LLC)

Videos are embedded from YouTube. When you play a video, YouTube may set cookies and collect data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Gumroad

The free study bundle is hosted on Gumroad. If you download it, Gumroad collects your email address and processes the transaction (even at $0). See Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

GitHub Pages

This site may be hosted on GitHub Pages, which logs visitor IP addresses as part of standard web server operation. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Ko-fi

This site includes a link to Ko-fi for optional tips/donations. If you choose to support Tiago through Ko-fi, Ko-fi collects your payment information and processes the transaction. See Ko-fi's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log requests as described in their Google Fonts Privacy FAQ.

Your Rights

Since this site does not collect personal data, there is nothing to request, modify, or delete on our end. For data held by third-party services (YouTube, Gumroad, GitHub), please contact those services directly using the links above.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach Tiago through the YouTube channel.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for nursing students and healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
