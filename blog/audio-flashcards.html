How I use audio flashcards

Audio mode is not there so you can zone out. It is there so you can keep reviewing when your eyes are tired or when you are moving.

The loop

  1. Look at the card front and say what you remember.
  2. Flip the card and check the answer.
  3. Use audio to hear the explanation again.
  4. Mark the card based on how honest your recall was.

When it helps

  • Walking or commuting.
  • Repeating drug names out loud.
  • Reviewing weak cards after a practice set.
  • Getting one more pass when reading feels like too much.
