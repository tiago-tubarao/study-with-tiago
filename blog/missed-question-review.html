How I review a missed question

The point of a missed question is not to feel bad. The point is to find the exact place your thinking drifted.

My quick routine

  1. Name the topic: Was it content, priority, safety, or wording?
  2. Find the missed clue: What word in the question should have changed the answer?
  3. Write the one-line rule: Keep it short enough to become a flashcard.
  4. Make one new card: One card per miss. Do not write a textbook.
  5. Retest later: If it still feels shaky, it stays in the weak pile.
